Thesis & Dissertation Submittal System

Once you have passed the final defense and satisfied the requirements of the committee, you are ready to submit your manuscript to the Graduate School for review. This submittal process is fully electronic, and is made through an online application developed and maintained by the Texas Digital Library, in conjunction with the The University of Texas Libraries.

To get started with your submittal, click the link below. You will be asked to authenticate using your EID:

Start your submittal

For more information regarding graduation requirements, or to make an appointment to speak with a degree evaluator, please contact the Graduate Student Services Office at (512) 471-4511, or online at http://www.utexas.edu/ogs/.

Your help can make things better

You feedback is very important to us; it allows us to continue to improve the system. Please feel free to notify us directly at info@tdl.org if you have any suggestions to increase the usability or effectiveness of this application. You should contact the Graduate Student Services Office regarding any issues that may arise during the submittal process.
